Da' Braidr is a research engine designed to understand how Generstive AI might lead to the evolution of Black braiding processes-from identifying your hairstyle to locating a hair braider.

This project by Minne Atair builds on existing hair-related Generative AI projects: Fro Studies, Box Braids Study, Blonde Braids Study, Cornrow Studies, Ribbon Studies, Beaded Briads Studies.
