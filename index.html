Da Braidr is a multimodal research engine designed to explore how Generative AI might shape and support Black braiding communities. Begin by describing your imagined hairstyle in a text prompt. Da Braidr will translate your description into a visual representation alongside an analysis of the braid pattern, and structure. Finally, text Da Braidr to connect you with an expert human braider who understands who will realize your vision.

This project by Minne Atairu builds on existing hair-related Generative AI projects: Fro Studies (2022), Box Braids Study (2022), Blonde Braids Study (2022), Cornrow Studies (2022), Ribbon Studies (2022), Beaded Braids Studies (2022).

TECHNICAL DESCRIPTION
Da Braidr leverages the vision-language capabilities of OpenAI models, fine-tuned specifically for the task of braid pattern recognition The system's vision model is fine-tuned using diverse, open-source datasets that prominently feature braiding styles. We have also established a dedicated portal to receive user-contributed image data, currently accepting only non-synthetic images to maintain authenticity and quality in representation.
